Choose Tidy options and view descriptions

Tidy offers many options for cleaning your HTML, correcting errors, identifying accessibility issues, and more. All of the available options are available in the document window pane labeled “Tidy Options,” as shown to the left.

If you select any of the Tidy options, a description of that option appears at the bottom of the Tidy Options panel.

Some of the descriptions are quite long, and so you may choose to hide the descriptions. This is easily done by clicking on the “DESCRIPTION” label in the options panel. Clicking it again will show the descriptions again.
